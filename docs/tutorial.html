RangerFS in 5 minutes

Firstly reference RangerFS.dll from Nuget and then reference the .dll in F# Interactive and open the Ranger namespace.

1: 
2: 
3: 
4: 

#r "RangerFS.dll"

open Ranger
open Ranger.Operators

Constructing Ranges

The primary constructor of Range<'t> is Range.ofBounds or the equivalent operator <=>

1: 
2: 
3: 
4: 

let a = Range.ofBounds 0 10
let b = 0 <=> 10

printf "%O is equal to %O" a b

Arguments to Range.ofBounds may be provided in any order

1: 
2: 
3: 
4: 

let c = Range.ofBounds 2 10
let d = Range.ofBounds 10 2

The special case where range.Lo = range.Hi is referred to as a Point and can be constructed by Range.ofPoint or it's operator equivalent !

2:

4:

// The following are all equivalent
let e = Range.ofPoint 2.5
let f = !2.5
let g = Range.ofBounds 2.5 2.5

printf "%O = %O = %O" e f g
